Exploring Architectural Floor Plans

Kitchen Design

In residential architecture, the kitchen serves as a central hub for family activities. Effective designs incorporate functional layouts that maximize space, such as using slabs for countertops and integrating storage solutions to meet daily needs.

Garage and Living Room Integration

Modern floor plans often emphasize seamless integration of spaces like garages with living areas. This approach applies principles of design to create efficient, eco-friendly homes, drawing from concepts like Georgian home styles and sustainable practices.
